CHILD MALTREATMENT DATABASE SYSTEM

FAQS

Some commonly asked questions

1. What is child Maltreatment Database System?
Ans:Child maltreatment database system is a database system created for helping some childrens who are victimised with various abuses.CMDS will help the child to lodge a complaint which will be registered in the database operated by higher authority.

2. How to lodge complaint at CMDS?
Ans:In CMDS on homepage we have created a lodge complaint where victims name,city,email address,mobile,abuse type faced by child and once you submit the complaint will be successfully registered.

3. How to track my complaint status?
Ans:If you have lodged a complaint you will receive a complaint number so details filled in lodge complaint are to be filled in track status with complaint number and you will be able to track the details of the child.you will be able to check at which desk your case has reached in track status.

4. How to sent queries?
Ans:In CMDS,Navigation bar mentioned above contact us is mentioned where you have to write your Name,email address,subject and message.In message you have to write your queries.

5. Who can report child abuse or neglect?
Ans: Child abuse and neglect can be reported by victim as well as guardian.

6. What is the procedure of solving case?
Ans: in track status complete overview of the case is mentioned.Desk 1 confirms that case is received an it processed now.Desk 2 confirms that documents are verified.Desk 3 confirms documents are further transfered to Desk 4.Desk 4 confirms that procedure is completed.

7. How much time it would approximately take to solve case?
Ans:It depends as the desk is processed step by step.
